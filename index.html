Title: Overlapping Protein Complex Detection Based on Multimodal Data Integration

Supervisor: Md. Shahidul Islam | University of Asia Pacific

Abstract

Addressed limitations of traditional "hard clustering" in bioinformatics by proposing a Deep Learning-based framework to detect overlapping protein complexes using multi-modal biological data.

Key Contributions

  • Developed a novel feature fusion technique combining Network Topology (Node2Vec), Amino Acid Composition (AAC), and Physicochemical properties.
  • Implemented an Adaptive Neural Network clustering approach that identified 80 optimal clusters without manual tuning.

Outcome & Tech Stack

  • Accuracy: Achieved 97.78% Protein-level Accuracy and 0.989 F1-Score.
  • Tech: Python, TensorFlow, NetworkX, Scikit-learn, Node2Vec, Pandas.
